About the Skill

The Plant Alexa Skill is your ultimate companion for managing your plant collection. With this skill, you can effortlessly store information about your plants and add them to Alexa. Personalize each plant by setting its type and name, and keep track of watering schedules by letting Alexa know when you've watered your plants.

How to Use

Enable the skill on your Amazon Alexa device by saying:

"Alexa, enable New Plant."

Once enabled, you can use commands like:

  • "Alexa, my new plant is a english ivy."
  • "Alexa, I have watered my plant."
  • "Alexa, name my plant."
  • "Alexa, add a new plant to my home.
